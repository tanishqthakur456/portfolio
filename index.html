About Me

A storyteller at
heart & hand

I'm Tanishq Thakur, a creative pursuing a Bachelor of Design in Animation & VFX at the Asian Academy of Film & Television (AAFT), Noida.

My practice spans traditional pencil portraiture to digital poster design and motion work — I believe the best visuals are born where craft meets concept. Each piece begins with observation: the weight of a shadow, the tension of a gaze, the kinetic energy of a moment.

Whether sketching faces with a grid and graphite or building compositions in Photoshop and After Effects, I bring the same attention to structure, light, and narrative to every frame.
